How We Build Your Website

Four clear phases. You'll know exactly what's happening and what's needed from you at every step.

01

Discovery

50% deposit to reserve your start date

We hop on a call to understand your business, your audience, and what you need the site to do. You'll send over your content, branding, and any references you like. No payment until scope is confirmed — then we send a Stripe invoice link.

02

Design

2 design revision rounds included

We create a visual mockup of your website so you can see exactly how it will look before we write any code. You get revision rounds to refine it until it feels right.

03

Build

Design approved & locked in

Once you approve the design, we build it with clean, hand-written code. No templates, no page builders. Your site is tested across devices and browsers before you ever see it.

04

Launch

Remaining 50% due before launch

After your approval on staging, the final 50% is due — then your site goes live. We'll walk you through how to manage it and provide 14 days of post-launch support for any questions or minor fixes.
