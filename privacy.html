Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Introduction

AlgoCraftDev (“we,” “us,” or “our”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data.

Information We Collect

When you use our contact form, we collect the following information:

  • Name — to address you personally in our response
  • Email address — to reply to your inquiry
  • Message content — the details you provide about your project
  • Service and package selections — choices you make on our website to help us understand your needs

We do not collect passwords, payment information, or any sensitive personal data through this website.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiry or project request
  • Provide you with a project quote or consultation
  • Communicate about services you’ve expressed interest in

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this website:

  • EmailJS — to process and deliver contact form submissions. Your name, email, and message are transmitted through their service. See EmailJS Privacy Policy.
  • Google Fonts — to load website typography. Google may collect your IP address. See Google Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudflare Turnstile — to protect our contact forms from spam. See Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

Cookies & Local Storage

This website does not use cookies for tracking or advertising. We use sessionStorage (a browser feature) to temporarily remember your service and package selections as you navigate the site. This data:

  • Is stored only in your browser
  • Is never sent to any server
  • Is automatically cleared when you close your browser tab

Data Retention

Contact form submissions are delivered to our email inbox and retained only as long as needed to respond to your inquiry and complete any resulting project. We do not maintain a separate database of form submissions.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of any personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for communication at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@algocraftdev.com.

Children’s Privacy

This website is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at support@algocraftdev.com.
